About


Mercedes-Benz is a legendary German automotive brand globally renowned for luxury, engineering excellence, and safety innovation. Founded in 1926 by visionaries Carl Benz and Gottlieb Daimler, the company is the oldest car manufacturer in the world.

History & LegacyOrigins:
  • The name "Mercedes" was inspired by the daughter of Emil Jellinek, an automotive dealer who played a massive role in the brand's early commercial success.
  • The Logo: The iconic three-pointed star represents the brand's ambition to dominate land, sea, and air with their engineering prowess.
  • Safety Firsts: Mercedes-Benz was the pioneer of the crumple zone, ABS (Anti-lock Braking System), and the modern airbag.
